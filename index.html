The man behind the mission -

Dr. Sanjay Pattnaik -

Sanjay has a humble beginning. Coming from the holy town of Jagannath Puri, in Odisha, he is culturally ingrained with two powerful thoughts, “belief in self and help others to believe in themselves”. These are the two powerful beliefs, acted as the prime movers all through his life, whether it is in his employment stint or in the entrepreneurial journey, he has embarked upon.

We help define Mission, Vision, and Values through discovery workshops and embed them into the organization's DNA. Our services also include organization design, restructuring, and shaping workplace culture to foster collaboration and accountability.

PeopleBetter is his initiative with a strong conviction that each person in this earth has an aspiration to grow and is naturally blessed with qualities that helps him to realize his dream. The tricky part is, does he really know his potential and use it to his advantage. One needs an enabler who can help ignite the fire in him to get him closer to his goal. Helping individuals and enlighten them with their strength, is in fact an eureka moment. PepoleBetter endeavors to bring these moments in everybody’s life.

Sanjay is alum of Symbiosis, and a PhD in area of management. He carries the idea of diversity in everything, which is where the ideas clash, bounce and blossom for the better.

What matters is “Staying Eager and Become Better.
